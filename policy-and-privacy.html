Privacy Policy

Introduction

At idlebreakout-game.github.io, we prioritize your privacy and the protection of your personal data above all else. Information We Collect

To enhance your experience and improve our services, we collect certain types of information, which include:

  • Personal Information: This includes any data you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and contact details. This information may be collected when you subscribe to newsletters, register an account, submit inquiries, participate in surveys, or otherwise communicate with us.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized for multiple purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Operating, maintaining, and optimizing the performance of idlebreakout-game.github.io and the associated gaming services.
  • Communicating with you regarding updates, promotions, newsletters, customer support responses, and other relevant notifications.
  • Personalizing your experience by tailoring content, game recommendations, and offers according to your preferences and interests.
  • Analyzing user behavior and trends to enhance security, detect fraudulent activities, and improve overall user satisfaction.
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and other relevant policies.
